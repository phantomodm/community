What is JamTech?

JamTech is a organization whos mission is to connect business and workers to ongoing and future projects happening in Jamaica NYC.

Our goal is to create a network of local business and professionals preparing them to bid, win and excecute ongoing and future projects, located within Jamaica NYC.

Hello, I'm Isa

Executive Director

My name is Isa Abdur-Rahman, Esq. I am the Executive Director of the Farmers Boulevard Community Development Corp. I've lived here in the city of Jamaica from my youth. I've witness all the changes that has happened over the years. There has been alot of change that has been good and a lot of change that could be better.

As a practicing attorney, I represent entrepreneurs, project managers and individuals who have difficulty finding minority owned businesses and professionals, ready to qualified local talent to fill positions for ongoing opportunities.
